JUNO ESTEVEZ

Hello, my name is Juno Estevez, and I welcome you to my resume.
I was born and raised in Florida, and I take classes in Seminole State College, though I plan to major in Studio Art at the University of Central Florida.
Throughout my life, my passion has been with animation and character design, as long as I can remember, though I've also learned about various other art forms.
My dream has always been to create a series of cartoons or comics that can properly see its conclusion and make an impact with its fanbase.
Having browsed the internet for most of my life, I found various kinds of animations and browser games that enraptured me as a child.
I aim to create works of art that can impact an audience the same way I was.
